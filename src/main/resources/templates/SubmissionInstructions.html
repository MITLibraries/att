Submission Instructions

  1. Click the Submit records button
  2. First, select "MIT Thesis 2020" from the dropdown menu below.
  3. Click the Next: Record details button.
  4. Enter the Date span of the records, Department, Degree(s), and Number of Theses.
  5. Once finished, click Next: Select files
  6. Upload your digital content on the Add Digital Content Files page. Click the Choose File button under Select a file to upload , which will open your computerâ€™s file browser.
    • If you have multiple files or folder(s), you will have to put them into a zip folder first. To create a zip folder, follow these instructions for PC and OSX.
    • Name the zip file DeptProgramName_degree_2020_May_theses.
    • If you select the wrong file, you can click Remove all files to start over.
  7. Click the Submit Query button to upload your files. As the page notes, this can take some time. Do not close your Browser window until the files have completed their upload.
  8. Once upload is complete, the tool will bring you to the next page and provide a summary of what you submitted. We will also contact you to confirm the successful transfer of your digital material.
