Submission Instructions

  1. Click the Submit records button
  2. First, select your DLC or the donor record so we can match your digital material with an existing submission agreement. Don't see your name or DLC on the list? Please contact us.
  3. Click the Next: Record details button.
  4. Add the starting year and end year of the digital material to the best of your knowledge. If all of the files are from a single year, record that in the starting year and leave the end year blank.
  5. Add a brief description about the person or office that created the material, or any special considerations regarding the material such as comments on access restrictions.
  6. The next step is to upload the file. At the bottom of the page you will see information from your completed submission agreement. This will be pre-filled based on prior contact with the Department of Distinctive Collections. If it looks incorrect, please contact us.
  7. Once finished, click Next: Select file to select the file on your computer.
  8. Upload your digital content on the Add Digital Content File page (this may be a zip folder). Click the Choose File button under Select a file to upload , which will open your computerâ€™s file browser. If you select the wrong file, you can click Remove file to start over.
  9. If you have multiple files or folder(s), you will have to put them into a zip folder first. To create a zip folder, follow these instructions for PC and OSX.
  10. Click the Submit button to upload your files. This may take some time, so please leave the page open while the files upload
  11. Once upload is complete, the tool will bring you to the next page and provide a summary of what you submitted. Please save or print the confirmation page. It will not be emailed to you. We will also contact you to confirm the successful transfer of your digital material.

Questions? Contact us.
